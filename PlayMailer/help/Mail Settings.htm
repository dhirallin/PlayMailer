Mail Settings

To use PlayMailer you need an email account (from your ISP, GMail or other provider), then configure PlayMailer with your Mail Settings..

Basic Account Information

  • Your Name -- This is the default name that will be shown in player lists and in chat messages (when no player name is provided for a Session.)
  • Your Email -- Your email address.

Incoming Mail Settings

  • Incoming Mail Server -- Your email provider's IMAP/POP3 server address.
  • Mail Protocol -- IMAP or POP3. IMAP is strongly recommended if supported by your mail server, since it is much faster than POP3 and won't interfere with your regular emails. If your mail server doesn't support IMAP, then the best option is to create a new email account with a free provider such as GMail. Instructions for configuring PlayMailer for GMail are provided at the end of this page.
  • Security Protocol -- TLS, SSL or None. Many web based email providers (e.g. Gmail) require that you use SSL encryption for incoming mail.  
  • Login Name -- Your email account login name.
  • Password --  Your email account password.
  • Check email every ... seconds -- This settings is only used with POP3 and older (< v4) versions of IMAP (IMAPv4 does not use polling.). By default PlayMailer checks for email every 60 seconds with POP3 and every 30 seconds with IMAP v3 or lower.

Outgoing Mail Settings

  • Outgoing Mail Server --  Your email provider's SMTP server address (possibly the same as your Incoming Mail Server.)
  • Security Protocol -- TLS, SSL or None. Many web based email providers require that you use some kind of encryption.
  • Same as Incoming Mail Settings -- Tick this option to use the same login and password for Incoming and Outgoing mail servers.
  • Login Name -- Your outgoing mail server account login name.
  • Password --  Your outgoing mail server account password.

Example: Using GMail with PlayMailer

To use a GMail account with PlayMailer:
Firstly you need to enable IMAP from the GMail website.
Open a browser. Go to the GMail site and login to your email account.
Now go to GMail Settings (Click on the cog-wheel in the top-right of the page, and select 'Settings'.
Click on 'Forwarding and POP/IMAP' and set 'Enable IMAP' then Save Changes.

Now in PlayMailer, go to Mail Settings and enter the following:
Incoming Mail Settings - imap.gmail.com, IMAP, SSL and your gmail login and password.
Outgoing Mail Settings - smtp.gmail.com, TLS, tick 'Same as Incoming Mail Settings'.
