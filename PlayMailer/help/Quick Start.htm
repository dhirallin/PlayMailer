Quick Start

Pre-requisites

1) PlayMailer does not come bundled with any games. You will need to seperately acquire games that you wish to play with PlayMailer. Most games supported by PlayMailer can be downloaded from Abandonware sites.
2) If you intend to play MS-DOS games, you will also need to install the latest version of DOSBox from http://www.dosbox.com/. DOSBox is a free MS-DOS emulator.

Step 1 - Configure Mail Settings:

1) Click on the Settings button at the top of the PlayMailer window.
2) On the left, select the Mail Settings page.
3) Enter your name, email address, and mail server settings and press OK.
Note: It is strongly recommended that you use IMAP as your Incoming Mail Protocol (if supported by your mail server). POP3 is much slower and may cause PlayMailer to interfere with your regular mail. If your mail server doesn't support IMAP, then the best option is to create a new email account with a free provider such as GMail. Instructions for configuring PlayMailer for GMail are provided below.

Example: Using GMail with PlayMailer

To use a GMail account with PlayMailer:
Firstly you need to enable IMAP from the GMail website.
Open a browser. Go to the GMail site and login to your email account.
Now go to GMail Settings (Click on the cog-wheel in the top-right of the page, and select 'Settings'.
Click on 'Forwarding and POP/IMAP' and set 'Enable IMAP' then Save Changes.

Now in PlayMailer, go to Mail Settings and enter the following:
Incoming Mail Settings - imap.gmail.com, IMAP, SSL and your gmail login and password.
Outgoing Mail Settings - smtp.gmail.com, TLS, tick 'Same as Incoming Mail Settings'.

Step 2 - Create a New Session:

1) Click on the New Session button.
2) Under Session Type, select the game you wish to play.
3) Enter a unique name for your session.
4) Click on Add / Edit Players.
5) Add yourself to the game by clicking on Add Local Player.
6) Enter a Player Name for yourself and select a Team for your new player.
7) Add other remote players by clicking on Add Player, or add more local players with Add Local Player.
8) Enter the Email Address, Player Name (optional) and Team for each new player.
Note: If you set a player's team to Player's Choice then a request will be sent to that player asking them to choose their own team.
9) Click on OK.
10) At the bottom of the Session Settings window, set any other game-specific settings as desired. IMPORTANT: Do not change any of these settings from within your game. Only change them from PlayMailer, This ensures the settings are synchronised between players.
11) Click on OK.

Step 3 - Configure Game Paths:

At this point a window may appear asking you to configure the path to your game folder (if you have not already done so.)
1) Download the game you intend to play from an Abandonware site or other source and unpack it into a location on your computer.
2) Enter the path to the game folder under Game Folder Path. You can press the [...] button to browse folders.
3) Press OK.
4) If the game requires DOSBox and it is not currently installed, another window will appear asking you to download and install DOSBox. Once you have done this, press OK. If DOSBox cannot be found, you will be asked to manually select your DOSBox install folder.

Step 4 - Have your First Turn

Once you have created a new session, an email will be sent to all players inviting them to join the session. Players will then be asked to enter a Player Name and any other required player settings. Players can also modify the game's settings at this time or edit the player list, e.g. to add additional players..
1) Wait until it is your turn. A window will popup informing you when it is your turn.
2) Click the Load Now button and wait for the game to load fully.
Note: PlayMailer will automatically setup the game, configure all players and load your turn for you.
Important: Do not use your mouse or keyboard while PlayMailer is loading the game. 
3) Have your first turn. But do not press 'End Turn' at the end of your turn.
4) When you are finished your turn press the following Hotkey Ctrl + Shift + S from within the game window to save the game and send it to the next player.
5) Wait until your next turn. You may close the game or switch windows if you desire.
Note: If you ever accidently press End Turn in your game, you will have to re-load the game from the PlayMailer window and redo your turn.

Step 5 - Sending Chat Messages

To send a chat message to All Players in the game - Press Ctrl + Shift + M and type in your message. 
To send a chat message to a single player, or a selection of players - Press Ctrl + Shift + P.  Tick the players you want to send a message to and press Send Message To Selected.

You can view other available in-game hotkeys by pressing Ctrl + Shift + H.

Enjoy!
